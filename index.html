How It Started

1958

Establishment of DOST

The Department of Science and Technology (DOST) was established as the National Science and Development Board (NSDB) on June 13, 1958. It was later reconstituted as the National Science and Technology Authority (NSTA) in 1981 and elevated to cabinet-level status in 1987, becoming the DOST we know today.

2000s

Expansion of Services

Over the years, DOST expanded its services to include various programs aimed at fostering innovation, supporting education, and enhancing disaster risk reduction efforts across the Philippines.
